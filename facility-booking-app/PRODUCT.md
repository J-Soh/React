# SAF Facility Booking

## Product Purpose
Internal facility booking system for SAF (Singapore Armed Forces) personnel. Allows staff to reserve rooms, equipment, and shared spaces. Managers approve or reject requests; clients submit and track bookings.

## Register
product

## Users
- **Staff (clients):** SAF personnel booking facilities for meetings, training, events. Familiar with internal tools, no patience for friction. Using on office desktops during work hours.
- **Managers (boss):** Approve/reject booking requests, manage facility inventory. Need quick triage, not deep analysis.

## Brand & Tone
- Professional, utilitarian, institutional. Not playful, not startup-y.
- Clarity over personality. Information density over decoration.
- Military-adjacent: structured, direct, no-nonsense.

## Anti-references
- SaaS marketing dashboards with gradient cards and confetti
- Glassmorphism-heavy "futuristic" UIs
- Dark-mode-for-cool dashboards with neon accents
- Overly animated onboarding flows

## Strategic Principles
- Speed of task completion over visual impressiveness
- Status visibility: always know where a booking stands
- Minimal clicks to book or approve
